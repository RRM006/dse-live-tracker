Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026

DSE Live Tracker ("the App") is a stock tracking application that provides real-time price information from the Dhaka Stock Exchange (DSE). This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your data.

Data Collection

The App does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data. We do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Device identifiers or location data
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Any other personally identifiable information

Local Storage

All data you enter into the App (stock symbols, buy prices, quantities, and watchlist items) is stored entirely on your device using local storage. This data never leaves your device.

Internet Access

The App requires internet access solely to fetch publicly available stock price data from the Dhaka Stock Exchange website (dsebd.org). No user data is transmitted during this process.

Third-Party Services

The App uses a CORS proxy service (corsproxy.io) to facilitate data retrieval from the DSE website. This proxy only sees the URL of the public data being fetched and does not receive any user information.

Notifications

The App may display local push notifications when a watched stock reaches a user-defined target price. These notifications are generated entirely on your device. No data is sent to any external server.

Data Security

Since all personal data remains on your device, the security of that data is managed by your device's built-in security features. We recommend securing your device with a screen lock.

Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy is updated, the "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the change. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
